## Add a One Time Payment Product

Merchants can create a new One-Time Payment Product for goods or services that customers pay for once, making it ideal for digital goods and e-books

**How to Add a One-Time Payment Product:**

1. **Navigate to the Dashboard**:
    - Log in to your Dodo Payments dashboard.
2. **Go to the Products Section**:
    - On the sidebar menu, click on **Products** to view your current catalogue.
3. **Add a New Product**:
    - Click on the **Add New Product** button.
    - Choose the **One-Time Payment Product** option from the dropdown.
4. **Enter Product Details**:
    - Fill in the product's basic details:
        - **Product Name**: Provide a clear and descriptive name.
        - **Description**: Add a detailed explanation of the product or service. This should include any relevant features, specifications, or benefits.
        - **Price**: Set a one-time price for the product.
        - **Discount**: Set the % discount to be offered for the product
        - **Product Category** : Select the product category to help us identify the tax category it belongs to
5. **Add Media**:
    - Upload images to visually represent the product
6. **Save Your Product**:
    - Once all the details are complete, click **Save** to add the product to your catalogue. It will now be available for customers to purchase.

## Edit a One Time Payment Product
You can update or modify any details of an existing one-time payment product at any time, such as the price, description, or visuals, without affecting past sales. Changes you make will only apply to future purchases.

**How to Edit an Existing Product:**

1. **Access the Products Section**:
    - From your dashboard, navigate to the **Products** section to view all your products.
2. **Locate the Product to Edit**:
    - Find the product you want to modify in the list and click on the edit button
3. **Make Changes**:
    - Click on the product and update the necessary details:
        - **Price**: Adjust the one-time price if necessary.
        - **Description**: Modify or add any additional information about the product.
        - **Media**: Update images or videos to better reflect the current state of the product.
4. **Save Changes**:
    - After making your edits, click **Save** to apply the changes.

<Info>Changes will only affect future sales. Past transactions and purchases will remain unaffected by any modifications.</Info>

## Delete a One Time Payment Product

If you decide to discontinue a product or if itâ€™s no longer relevant to your offerings, you can easily remove it from your Product Catalogue. Deleting a product ensures that it can no longer be purchased by new customers, but existing sales records remain unaffected.

**How to Delete a One-Time Payment Product:**

1. **Access the Products Section**:
    - Go to the **Products** section of your dashboard.
2. **Find the Product to Delete**:
    - Scroll through your list of products or use the search bar to locate the product you want to delete.
3. **Delete the Product**:
    - Click the **Delete** button next to the product name.
    - Confirm the deletion when prompted.

<Info>Once deleted, the product cannot be recovered. Deleting a product does not affect customers who have already purchased the product.</Info>
