## Step 1: Access the Products Section

1. In the dashboard, locate and click on the **Products.**
2. Create One time product by providing the necessary information.
You can refer this link on how you can create an onetime product here
https://docs.dodopayments.com/guides/one-time-product

## Step 2: Configure Your Payment Link

1. After creating a product, click on Share button under Payment link
    - Provide the quantity
    - Provide the Redirect URL - (to where your customer should be redirected after a payment)
2. Copy the Payment Link and you can share it to your customer to receive payments.
    
    Please refer this guide for step by step process
    https://docs.dodopayments.com/guides/payment-links-guide
    

Dodo Payments makes it simple to create and share payment links without any coding knowledge. Whether for one-time payments or recurring transactions, this feature streamlines the process and enhances your payment collection experience.
